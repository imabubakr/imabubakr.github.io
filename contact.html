GIS & RS

    GIS (Geographic Information Systems) and RS (Remote Sensing) are powerful tools that can be used to analyze and visualize spatial data. I have experience using a range of GIS and RS software, such as ArcGIS, QGIS, SNAP and ENVI, and can integrate these tools with other data sources to gain a comprehensive understanding of spatial patterns and trends. Whether it's creating maps for planning purposes, conducting spatial analysis to identify hotspots, or using remote sensing techniques to monitor environmental changes, I can leverage these skills to provide valuable solutions and support to a range of projects.
