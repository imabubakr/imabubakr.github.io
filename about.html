About Me

During my final year of undergrad, I discovered my passion for GIS and Remote Sensing, which led me to pursue a Master's degree in the field. Through my academic projects and part-time work as a GIS analyst, I have applied my knowledge to real-world scenarios, gaining valuable experience.

Later, I pursued a Master's degree in Geology in Germany, where I continued to apply my remote sensing expertise to the field. After completing my studies, I worked as a geospatial data analyst for a consulting firm, where I collaborated on various projects and contributed to the development of GIS solutions.

With three degrees and a range of project experience, I am a skilled professional in geospatial data analysis. In my free time, I enjoy exploring the Black Forest, trying local cuisine, and learning new languages. I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges, and I look forward to what the future holds in the exciting field of geospatial data analysis.
