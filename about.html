About Me

My journey in GIS and Remote Sensing began during my undergraduate studies in Geology, where I discovered how spatial data could address real-world challenges. During my graduate studies, I honed advanced skills in image analysis, geospatial statistics, and spatial databases, applying Earth Observation data to study climate variability.

I have experience in GIS and Remote Sensing analysis, managing spatial data in Geodatabases, developing geospatial models, and creating custom maps. I've collaborated with clients from industries such as urban planning, natural resource management, and academia, delivering innovative spatial solutions.

I am passionate about leveraging machine learning, deep learning, and Web GIS development to advance spatial science. With a strong interest in data visualization and open data, I continually explore new tools and techniques to enhance my work.
